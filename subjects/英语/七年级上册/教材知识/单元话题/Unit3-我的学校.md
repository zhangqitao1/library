# Unit 3 My School 话题功能：我的学校

## 概念 / 定义
本单元学**介绍学校设施、描述某物在哪里**。语法主线是 **There be 句型** + **方位介词** + **some/any、how many/how much**。是写"描述类"作文的关键单元。

## 公式 · 规则 · 结论（要能直接背）
- **学校设施词**：classroom, library, playground, office, lab（实验室）, building, gate, hall, dining hall, art room, music room。
- **核心交际句型**：
  - There is a library in our school.
  - There are many trees on the playground.
  - Is there a lab in your school? — Yes, there is.
  - How many classrooms are there? — There are 20.
  - It's on the second floor.（在二楼）
  - The library is between the classroom and the playground.
- **方位介词**：in / on / under / behind / in front of / near / next to / between...and... / on the left(right)。
- **配套语法**：there be 就近原则、some/any、how many/much、方位介词（全在 06 卡）。

## 典型例题
> 例：— ___ there a music room in your school? — Yes, ___ ___. It's on the third floor.
>
> 解：— **Is** there a music room...? — Yes, **there is**. It's on the third floor.

## ⚠️ 易错点 · 陷阱
- ❌ Our school **has** a big playground 与 ✅ There **is** a big playground in our school——两种说法都对，但别把结构混成 "There has..."。
- ❌ How **much** classrooms ✅ How **many** classrooms（可数复数用 many）
- on the second floor = 在二楼（floor 用序数词 + the）。
- 描述顺序作文常用：方位词 + there be，注意 be 跟最近名词一致。

## 变式 · 拓展 · 中考链接
- 写作：用 5–6 句 there be + 方位介词介绍自己的学校或房间。
- 中考完形高频：there be 与 have 的转换、就近原则、some/any。
