# Unit 1 Animal Friends

## 话题
### 动物种类
- 宠物 pet：dog, cat
- 野生动物：panda, tiger, dolphin
### 动物特征
- cute / friendly / shy / dangerous
### 动物保护与栖息地
- protect / endangered / habitat / zoo

## 语法
### 特殊疑问句
- What animals...?
- Where do they live?
- What do they eat?
- Why do you like...?
### 形容词
- 修饰名词 / be + adj.
### 名词复数（复习）
- tiger→tigers, wolf→wolves

## 核心句型
- I like... because they are...
- We should protect...

## 写作
- My Favourite Animal
