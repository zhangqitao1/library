# Unit 5 Here and Now

## 话题
### 此刻正在做的事
- read / watch / cook / shop
### 打电话
- What are you doing?
### 现场描述
- Look! / Listen!

## 语法：现在进行时
### 结构
- am/is/are + v-ing
### 动词-ing规则
- +ing / 去e+ing / 双写+ing
### 标志词
- now / right now / at the moment

## 问答
- Are you watching TV?
- Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.
