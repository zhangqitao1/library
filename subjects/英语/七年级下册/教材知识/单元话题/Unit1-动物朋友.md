# Unit 1 Animal Friends 话题功能：动物朋友

## 概念 / 定义
本单元围绕**动物**展开：宠物、野生动物、动物园、动物习性，以及**为什么喜欢/如何保护动物**。语法配合：特殊疑问句、形容词描述、名词复数复习。

## 公式 · 规则 · 结论（要能直接背）
- **核心交际句型**：
  - What's your favourite animal? — I like **pandas** because they are **cute**.
  - What animals do you like? / Which animal do you like best?
  - Why do you like...? — Because they are friendly / smart.
  - Where do they live? — They live in the forest / in China.
  - What do they eat? — They eat bamboo / meat / plants.
- **核心词汇**（⚠️待核对课本 Section 词表）：
  - 动物：panda, tiger, elephant, dolphin, penguin, wolf, fox, giraffe, koala
  - 特征：cute, friendly, shy, scary, dangerous, smart, lazy
  - 其他：zoo, pet, wildlife, protect, endangered, habitat
- **写作框架**：My Favourite Animal — 名称→外貌（形容词）→习性（eat/live）→为什么喜欢

## 典型例题
> 例：写一句介绍熊猫。— Pandas ___ in China and they ___ bamboo.
>
> 解：Pandas **live** in China and they **eat** bamboo.（一般现在时表事实；三单 they 用原形）

## ⚠️ 易错点 · 陷阱
- ✗ I like **panda** → ✓ I like **pandas**（泛指用复数）
- ✗ The tiger is **danger** → ✓ ... **dangerous**
- ✗ They **lives** in the zoo → ✓ They **live**...（they 非三单）
- ✗ Why you like dogs? → ✓ **Why do you** like dogs?

## 变式 · 拓展 · 中考链接
- 与环保话题衔接：We should **protect** endangered animals.
- 阅读：动物习性匹配题（eat/live/look like）。

- Q: 问"熊猫吃什么"怎么说？ ｜ A: What do pandas eat?
- Q: 说"老虎很危险"怎么说？ ｜ A: Tigers are dangerous.
