# Unit 2 No Rules, No Order 话题功能：规则与秩序

## 概念 / 定义
本单元学**各种场合的规则**：学校、图书馆、博物馆、马路……没有规则就没有秩序。语法配合：祈使句、情态动词 can / have to / must。

## 公式 · 规则 · 结论（要能直接背）
- **核心交际句型**：
  - What are the school rules? — We **have to** wear uniforms. / We **must** be on time.
  - Can we ___? — Yes, you can. / No, you can't.
  - Don't ___! / You mustn't ___!
  - We should / shouldn't ___（应该/不应该，了解）
- **常见规则表达**：
  - Be quiet. / Don't run in the hallways.
  - No photos! / Don't eat or drink.
  - You have to / must do your homework.
  - You don't have to bring your phone.
- **核心词汇**（⚠️待核对）：rule, order, follow, obey, hall, hallway, uniform, on time, late, noise, quiet

## 典型例题
> 例：翻译"我们必须按时到校"。
>
> 解：We **must** / **have to** get to school **on time**.

## ⚠️ 易错点 · 陷阱
- ✗ You **mustn't** finish homework.（不必做）→ ✓ You **don't have to**...（mustn't 是禁止）
- ✗ **No** run! → ✓ **Don't run**! / **No running**!
- ✗ Can I **to** go out? → ✓ Can I **go** out?
- ✗ We **musts** wear... → ✓ We **must** wear...

## 变式 · 拓展 · 中考链接
- 写作：Class Rules（3–5 条，祈使句 + must/have to）。
- 与道德法治"规则意识"跨学科呼应。

## 一问一答（AI 复习时优先用这些来问她）
- Q: "不许在走廊跑"怎么说？ ｜ A: Don't run in the hallways.
- Q: have to 和 must 哪个更像"规定"？ ｜ A: 都可以；must 语气更强，mustn't 表禁止。
- Q: on time 什么意思？ ｜ A: 准时。
