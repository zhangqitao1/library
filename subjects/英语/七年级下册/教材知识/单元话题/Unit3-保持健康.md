# Unit 3 Keep Fit 话题功能：保持健康

## 概念 / 定义
本单元谈**运动、健身、健康生活习惯**：做什么运动、多久一次、为什么锻炼。语法配合：物主代词（尤其名词性）、频度副词、一般现在时三单复习。

## 公式 · 规则 · 结论（要能直接背）
- **核心交际句型**：
  - How often do you exercise? — **Twice a week** / **Every day**.
  - What sports do you play? — I play basketball / go swimming.
  - Do you often ___? — Yes, I **usually** do. / **Hardly ever**.
  - Whose racket is this? — It's **mine** / **yours**.
  - It's good for your health to ___（了解）
- **运动表达**：
  - play basketball/football/volleyball；go swimming/running/skating
  - do exercise / work out / keep fit / stay healthy
- **频度表达**：once/twice/three times a week；every day；on weekends
- **核心词汇**（⚠️待核对）：exercise, sport, healthy, health, fit, tennis, badminton, jogging

## 典型例题
> 例：— How often ___ you ___ (play) tennis? — Three times a week.
>
> 解：— How often **do** you **play** tennis?（习惯→一般现在时）

## ⚠️ 易错点 · 陷阱
- ✗ How **long** do you exercise?（问频率）→ ✓ How **often**...?
- ✗ I play basketball **one time** a week → ✓ **once** a week
- ✗ This is **my**. → ✓ This is **mine**.
- ✗ He **play** sports every day → ✓ He **plays**...

## 变式 · 拓展 · 中考链接
- 写作：My Healthy Lifestyle — 运动频率 + 好习惯。
- 阅读：图表题（根据表格答 How often...?）。

## 一问一答（AI 复习时优先用这些来问她）
- Q: "一周三次"英语怎么说？ ｜ A: three times a week。
- Q: go swimming 还是 play swimming？ ｜ A: **go** swimming（游泳用 go）。
- Q: hardly ever 什么意思？ ｜ A: 几乎从不。
