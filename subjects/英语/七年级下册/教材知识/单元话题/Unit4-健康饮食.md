# Unit 4 Eat Well 话题功能：健康饮食

## 概念 / 定义
本单元学**食物、点餐、均衡饮食**：喜欢吃什么、什么健康、餐厅对话。语法配合：选择疑问句、可数/不可数名词、would like。

## 公式 · 规则 · 结论（要能直接背）
- **核心交际句型**：
  - What would you like **to eat/drink**? — I'd like a bowl of noodles.
  - Would you like tea **or** coffee? — **Coffee**, please.
  - What's your favourite food? — I like...
  - How much/many ___ do you eat every day?
  - ___ is good/bad for your health.（了解）
- **点餐礼貌用语**：
  - Can I have...? / I'd like..., please.
  - For here or to go?（⚠️待核对是否出现在本册）
  - Anything else? — That's all. / No, thanks.
- **食物分类**：
  - 水果蔬菜：apple, banana, carrot, tomato...
  - 主食：rice, bread, noodles, dumplings...
  - 饮料：water, milk, juice, tea, coffee
- **核心词汇**（⚠️待核对）：meal, breakfast, lunch, dinner, snack, healthy, unhealthy, salt, sugar

## 典型例题
> 例：— Rice or noodles? — ___  （我要面条）
>
> 解：— **Noodles, please.** / **I'd like noodles.**

## ⚠️ 易错点 · 陷阱
- ✗ I'd like **a** water → ✓ **some** water / **a glass of** water
- ✗ — Tea or coffee? — **Yes**, tea. → ✓ **Tea**, please.
- ✗ How **many** rice → ✓ How **much** rice
- ✗ Would you like **eat**? → ✓ Would you like **to eat**?

## 变式 · 拓展 · 中考链接
- 写作：My Favourite Meal / A Healthy Diet。
- 中考听力：餐厅点餐对话是经典场景。

## 一问一答（AI 复习时优先用这些来问她）
- Q: "你想要点什么"礼貌说法？ ｜ A: What would you like to eat?
- Q: bread 可数吗？ ｜ A: 不可数；a piece of bread。
- Q: 选择疑问句怎么答？ ｜ A: 直接说选项，不用 Yes。
