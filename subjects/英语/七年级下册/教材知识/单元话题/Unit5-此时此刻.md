# Unit 5 Here and Now 话题功能：此时此刻

## 概念 / 定义
本单元聚焦**"现在正在做什么"**：打电话问对方在干嘛、描述眼前场景、逛市集/校园的即时活动。语法主线：**现在进行时（第一遍系统学习）**。

## 公式 · 规则 · 结论（要能直接背）
- **核心交际句型**：
  - What are you **doing** (now)? — I'm **reading** a book.
  - What is he/she **doing**? — He/She is **playing** basketball.
  - Are you **watching** TV? — Yes, I am. / No, I'm not. I'm doing homework.
  - Look! They are **having** a good time.
  - — Hello? — Hi, it's ___. What are you doing?
- **场景词汇**（⚠️待核对）：
  - 活动：read, watch, listen, cook, shop, chat, take photos
  - 地点：market, park, museum, café（⚠️拼写待核对）
- **电话/即时描述技巧**：用 Look!/Listen! 引出进行时

## 典型例题
> 例：— What ___ Tom ___ (do)? — He ___ (write) an email.
>
> 解：— What **is** Tom **doing**? — He **is writing** an email.

## ⚠️ 易错点 · 陷阱
- ✗ What **do** you doing? → ✓ What **are** you **doing**?
- ✗ She is **writeing**. → ✓ She is **writing**.（去 e +ing）
- ✗ They are **play** games now. → ✓ They are **playing**...
- ✗ I **amn't** doing... → ✓ I'm **not** doing...（口语缩写）

## 变式 · 拓展 · 中考链接
- 看图写话：描述图片中人物动作用现在进行时。
