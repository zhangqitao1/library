# Unit 7 A Day to Remember 话题功能：难忘的一天

## 概念 / 定义
本单元用**叙述过去经历**的方式学**一般过去时（一）**：上周末、一次旅行、学校活动、难忘的一天。重点是规则动词过去式和 did 句型。

## 公式 · 规则 · 结论（要能直接背）
- **核心交际句型**：
  - How was your weekend / trip / day? — It was **great/interesting/boring**.
  - What did you do **last weekend**? — I **visited** my grandparents.
  - Where did you go? — I went to the beach / museum.
  - Who did you go with? — I went with my parents.
  - Did you enjoy it? — Yes, I did. / No, I didn't.
- **时间标志词**：yesterday, last night/week/month/year, ... ago, in 2024, on Sunday
- **叙述框架**：时间 → 地点 → 人物 → 做了什么 → 感受
  - Last Sunday, I **went** to... We **played**... and **took** photos. It was fun!
- **核心词汇**（⚠️待核对）：trip, visit, camp, beach, museum, exciting, boring, wonderful

## 典型例题
> 例：用过去时填空：Yesterday I ___ (visit) the science museum and ___ (take) many photos.
>
> 解：Yesterday I **visited** ... and **took** ...（规则 +ed；take 不规则 took，U8 强化）

## ⚠️ 易错点 · 陷阱
- ✗ I **go** there yesterday. → ✓ I **went** there yesterday.
- ✗ **Did** you **went**? → ✓ **Did** you **go**?
- ✗ How **is** your weekend?（问过去）→ ✓ How **was** your weekend?
- ✗ Last week I **am** busy. → ✓ I **was** busy.

## 变式 · 拓展 · 中考链接
- 中考作文高频题：An Unforgettable Day / My Last Weekend。
- 与 Unit 8 故事叙述衔接，U7 偏"日记/经历"，U8 偏"童话/故事"。
