# Unit 8 Once Upon a Time 话题功能：从前有个故事

## 概念 / 定义
本单元以**讲故事、读童话/寓言**为语境，巩固**一般过去时（二）**：不规则动词、was/were、连贯叙述。开头常用 Once upon a time...（从前……）

## 公式 · 规则 · 结论（要能直接背）
- **故事开头/结尾**：
  - **Once upon a time**, there **was** a little boy...
  - One day, he **went** into the forest...
  - In the end, they **lived** happily ever after.（⚠️待核对课文原句）
- **核心交际句型**：
  - What happened **in the story**?
  - How did the story **end**?
  - What did the main character **do**?
  - The story **teaches** us to...（道理，可用一般现在时）
- **叙事连接词**：first, then, next, after that, finally, suddenly
- **核心词汇**（⚠️待核对）：story, character, king, queen, forest, brave, kind, wise, foolish

## 典型例题
> 例：连词成句（过去时）：the / boy / helped / old / an / man / kind
>
> 解：The boy **helped** a kind old man.

## ⚠️ 易错点 · 陷阱
- ✗ Once upon a time, there **is**... → ✓ there **was**...
- ✗ He **goed** home. → ✓ He **went** home.
- ✗ The story **was** teach us... → ✓ The story **teaches** us...（讲道理可用现在时）
- ✗ They **was** happy. → ✓ They **were** happy.

## 变式 · 拓展 · 中考链接
- 写作：续写故事 / 改写童话结局（全篇过去时）。
- 阅读：故事排序题（抓 first/then/finally）。

## 一问一答（AI 复习时优先用这些来问她）
- Q: 童话经典开头？ ｜ A: Once upon a time...
- Q: there was 还是 there were a boy? ｜ A: There **was** a boy.（a boy 单数）
- Q: go 的过去式？ ｜ A: went。
