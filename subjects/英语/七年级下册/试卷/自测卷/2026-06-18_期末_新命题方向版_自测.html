人教版2024 · 小雨 · 2026-06-18 · Unit 1-8
📌 用法：先读这页"怎么读、怎么写"的方法 → 合书复述三步法 → 再做下半部自测卷（不计分）。这套卷子重点不是"认语法"，而是"读懂语篇、提取信息、写好短文"。

一、长情境先提取 · 信息提取三步法（对接阅读/对话/完形）

步骤做什么给自己的口令
① 先看问题先读题目问什么，带着问题去读材料"题目要我找什么？"
② 回原文画词在材料里圈出对应的关键词/数字/时间"答案那句话在哪？圈出来"
③ 比对排除选项和原文比对，排除张冠李戴"原文真这么说吗？"
⚠️ 转折词最爱设陷阱：读到 but / however / although 要警觉——答案常在转折之后。

二、读写结合 · 完形顺逻辑（不要只看一个空）

  • 先把全文读一遍懂大意（讲谁、什么时候、发生了什么），再回头填空。
  • 顺着时间线 / 因果 / 转折选词：先定时态（标志词 now / Look! → 进行时；last / yesterday → 过去时；often / every day → 一般现在时），再看逻辑关系。

三、重过程规范 · 写作四步（书面表达提分关键）

步骤怎么做
① 审题圈出要点、人称、时态（介绍习惯→一般现在时；记上周末→一般过去时）
② 列点把每个要点列成短语，对着要点检查不漏点
③ 连句成段用衔接词把句子串起来：First / Second / Also / but / because / finally，别一句一句蹦
④ 检查时态一致、三单 -s、首字母大写、标点
🧠 评分标准记牢：要点全 + 句子连贯 + 时态人称对 + 卷面干净 > 辞藻华丽。

一、情境对话 · 信息提取（读对话选答案，每题2分，共10分）
先读懂对话在说什么，再到对话里找答案那一句。
1. A: Lily, what are you doing? You look busy.
B: I'm feeding my rabbit. It eats carrots every morning, but it's eating an apple now.
Q: What is Lily's rabbit eating now? [考点：现在进行时·信息提取·基础]
A. Carrots
B. An apple
C. Grass
D. Bread
答案与解析
B
题目问"现在(now)在吃什么"。对话里 every morning 吃胡萝卜是习惯，but…now 之后才是此刻：is eating an apple。看到 but/now 要定位到转折后的信息。
2. A: Mum, can I play computer games now?
B: No. You have to finish your homework first. After that you can play for half an hour.
Q: When can the boy play computer games? [考点：have to·情境理解·基础]
A. Right now
B. After he finishes his homework
C. Never
D. For two hours
答案与解析
B
妈妈说先(first)写完作业、之后(After that)才能玩。have to finish…first 是关键句，答案不是"现在"。
3. 天气广播："Good morning! It's cloudy in Jinan now, and it's going to rain in the afternoon. Take an umbrella when you go out."
Q: What should you take when you go out today? [考点：天气语篇·信息提取·基础]
A. Sunglasses
B. An umbrella
C. A coat
D. A hat
答案与解析
B
广播最后一句直接说 Take an umbrella。下午要下雨(rain)，所以带伞。回原文定位最后一句即可。
4. A: Whose schoolbag is this on the floor?
B: Oh, it's mine! Sorry, I'll put it away.
Q: Who does the schoolbag belong to? [考点：物主代词·信息提取·基础]
A. The first speaker
B. The second speaker
C. The teacher
D. Nobody
答案与解析
B
第二个人说 it's mine（=my schoolbag），所以书包是"第二个说话人"的。mine 是名词性物主代词。
5. A: Would you like some noodles or rice?
B: Rice, please. And a glass of orange juice.
Q: What does B want to drink? [考点：选择疑问句·点餐语篇·基础]
A. Tea
B. Water
C. Orange juice
D. Milk
答案与解析
C
题目问"喝(drink)什么"，rice 是吃的，要找"a glass of orange juice"。别被 rice 干扰，紧扣问题里的 drink。
二、单项选择（在语境中辨析，每题2分，共16分）
6. My little brother is only three, so he ___ go to school. He stays at home with Grandma. [考点：don't have to·语境·基础]
A. has to
B. doesn't have to
C. mustn't
D. must
答案与解析
B
三岁不用(不必)上学＝doesn't have to（不必）；mustn't 是"禁止上学"，不合语境。读懂后半句 stays at home 才能判断。
7. — The library is so quiet. — Yes, we ___ talk loudly here; it's the rule. [考点：mustn't 禁止·语境·基础]
A. don't have to
B. mustn't
C. needn't
D. can
答案与解析
B
图书馆规定"禁止"大声讲话＝mustn't；don't have to/needn't 是"不必"，意思不对。
8. Tom wants to keep fit, so he ___ goes to bed late. He sleeps early every day. [考点：频度副词词义·语境·中档]
A. always
B. usually
C. never
D. often
答案与解析
C
后一句 He sleeps early every day 说明他"从不(never)"晚睡。要靠下一句推出频度副词，不是单看一个空。
9. Look at the photo! In it, the children ___ on the beach while their parents ___ lunch. [考点：现在进行时·看图描述·基础]
A. play; cook
B. are playing; are cooking
C. played; cooked
D. play; are cooking
答案与解析
B
Look at the photo! 描述照片里正在发生的动作，两处都用现在进行时 are + v-ing。
10. My grandpa often ___ stories about his childhood, but today he ___ too tired to talk. [考点：一般现在时 vs 系动词·语境·中档]
A. tells; is
B. is telling; is
C. tells; was
D. told; is
答案与解析
A
often→习惯，一般现在时 tells（三单）；but today…是现在的状态，用 is。转折词 but 提示前后情况相反。
11. — How was your weekend? — Great! I ___ my grandparents and we ___ a big dinner together. [考点：一般过去时·不规则·语境·基础]
A. visit; have
B. visited; had
C. visit; had
D. visited; have
答案与解析
B
How was（过去）问周末，全程用过去时：visited（规则）、had（have 的不规则过去式）。
12. There isn't ___ milk in the fridge. Could you buy ___ for me? [考点：some/any·不可数·语境·中档]
A. some; any
B. any; some
C. any; any
D. some; some
答案与解析
B
否定句 isn't 用 any；第二句虽是疑问，但表"请求/希望对方答应"用 some（more polite）。milk 不可数，不加 s。
13. The story is about a poor boy. ___ he became a brave knight and saved the kingdom. [考点：故事衔接词·语篇·基础]
A. First
B. Finally
C. Suddenly
D. Because
答案与解析
B
从"穷男孩"到"成了勇敢骑士、拯救王国"是故事结局，用 Finally（最后）衔接最顺。讲故事衔接词：first→then→finally。
三、完形填空（先读懂全文再填，每题2分，共16分）
Last Saturday was a special day for me. In the morning, my family __14__ to the countryside by car. The weather __15__ sunny and warm. We had a picnic near a small river. After lunch, my father and I __16__ fishing, but we didn't catch anything!
In the afternoon, we __17__ a beautiful bird in a tree. My sister wanted to take a photo, so we all kept very __18__. __19__ we were a little tired, we felt very happy. I will never __20__ this wonderful day. Now I __21__ about it in my diary so that I can remember it forever.
14. [考点：完形·过去时·基础]
A. go
B. went
C. are going
D. goes
答案与解析
B
Last Saturday 全文是过去的事，go→went。
15. [考点：完形·be 过去式·基础]
A. is
B. was
C. were
D. are
答案与解析
B
The weather 是单数主语 + 过去时，用 was。
16. [考点：完形·过去时短语·基础]
A. go
B. goes
C. went
D. going
答案与解析
C
go fishing（去钓鱼）在过去时里用 went fishing。
17. [考点：完形·不规则过去式·基础]
A. see
B. saw
C. seen
D. seeing
答案与解析
B
"看见"一只鸟，过去时 see→saw（不规则）。
18. [考点：完形·形容词作表语·基础]
A. quiet
B. quietly
C. quick
D. loud
答案与解析
A
kept very ___ 中 keep 后接形容词，"保持安静"用 quiet（quietly 是副词，不能作表语）。拍照要安静，loud 不合逻辑。
19. [考点：完形·逻辑衔接·中档]
A. Because
B. Although
C. So
D. And
答案与解析
B
"虽然有点累，但很开心"是转折让步，用 Although。前后情绪相反是判断关键。
20. [考点：完形·词义逻辑·中档]
A. forget
B. remember
C. enjoy
D. know
答案与解析
A
"美好的一天我永远不会___"，且下一句说"写进日记好让我永远记住"，所以是 never forget（永不忘记）。
21. [考点：完形·时态转换 Now·中档]
A. write
B. wrote
C. am writing
D. writes
答案与解析
C
Now 提示此刻正在做，全文虽是过去时，这句切换到现在进行时 am writing。读完形要随逻辑/时间词换时态。
四、阅读理解（每题2分，共14分）
Welcome to the Green Animal Club!
Do you love animals? Come and join us! Our club helps to protect animals in our city.
Time: Every Saturday afternoon, 2:00-4:00
Place: Room 305, Building B
What we do:
  • We learn about different animals and why some of them are in danger.
  • We make posters to tell people not to hurt animals.
  • Once a month, we visit the city zoo to help the workers.
Rules: You must be on time. You mustn't bring food to the animals. You don't have to pay any money — it's free!
If you want to join, please call Mr. Green at 555-0188 before this Friday.
22. The Green Animal Club meets ___. [考点：阅读·细节·基础]
A. every morning
B. every Saturday afternoon
C. every day
D. once a year
答案与解析
B
Time 一行直接写 Every Saturday afternoon。细节题回原文定位 Time。
23. Club members do NOT ___. [考点：阅读·细节+推断·中档]
A. learn about animals
B. make posters
C. bring food to the animals
D. visit the zoo
答案与解析
C
规则里 You mustn't bring food to the animals（禁止喂食），所以"不做"的是 C。A/B/D 在 What we do 里都提到。
24. The words "in danger" probably mean ___. [考点：阅读·猜词·中档]
A. very safe
B. may die out
C. very strong
D. friendly
答案与解析
B
club 是"保护(protect)动物"的，"in danger"和保护相对，指动物"有危险、可能灭绝(die out)"。靠上下文 protect/hurt 猜词。
25. How much do you pay to join the club? [考点：阅读·细节·基础]
A. Nothing, it's free
B. A little money
C. 555 yuan
D. 305 yuan
答案与解析
A
规则里 You don't have to pay any money — it's free（免费）。别被 555/305 这些数字干扰，那是电话和房间号。
26. From the notice, we can learn that the club members ___. [考点：阅读·主旨推断·压轴]
A. only play games
B. care about protecting animals
C. don't like the zoo
D. sell animals for money
答案与解析
B
综合全文（保护动物、做海报、去动物园帮忙）可推断成员"关心保护动物"。这是概括推断题，要看整体而非一句。
27. If you want to join, you should call Mr. Green ___. [考点：阅读·细节·基础]
A. on Saturday
B. after Friday
C. before this Friday
D. anytime next month
答案与解析
C
最后一句 call…before this Friday。注意 before（之前），不是 after。
28. Lucy is often late. According to the rules, she needs to ___. [考点：阅读·规则应用·压轴]
A. bring some food
B. be on time
C. pay money
D. stay at home
答案与解析
B
把规则 You must be on time 应用到"Lucy 常迟到"这个新情境——她需要"守时(be on time)"。这是迁移应用题，把规则用到具体人身上。
五、情境补全对话（信息整合，每空2分，共8分）
从方框里选句子填进对话，使上下文连贯。多出一个选项不用。
方框选项：
A. Who are you going with?　B. What are you doing?　C. When are you leaving?　D. Remember to take some water.　E. How much is it?
Anna: Hi, Ben! __29__
Ben: I'm planning my weekend. I want to go hiking.
Anna: Sounds fun! __30__
Ben: I'm going with my cousin.
Anna: __31__
Ben: We're leaving at 7 o'clock on Saturday morning.
Anna: Wow, so early! __32__
Ben: Don't worry, I will. Thanks!
29. [考点：补全对话·现在进行时问句·基础]
答案与解析
B（What are you doing?）
下一句 I'm planning…回答"在做什么"，故问句是 What are you doing?
30. [考点：补全对话·信息整合·中档]
答案与解析
A（Who are you going with?）
回答 I'm going with my cousin（和表亲一起），对应问"和谁一起去"。
31. [考点：补全对话·信息整合·中档]
答案与解析
C（When are you leaving?）
回答 at 7 o'clock on Saturday morning（时间），对应问"什么时候出发"。
32. [考点：补全对话·交际·基础]
答案与解析
D（Remember to take some water.）
回答 Don't worry, I will（我会的），对应一句叮嘱"记得带水"。E（多少钱）与上下文无关，是干扰项。
六、书面表达（情境任务，15分）
33. 假设你是李华(Li Hua)。你的英国笔友 Mike 想知道你是怎么保持健康(keep fit)的。请根据要点给他回一封 60 词左右的短文。 [考点：情境化写作·读写结合·过程规范·压轴]
  • ① 你常做的运动（至少两项，说清 how often）；
  • ② 你的饮食习惯（eat well）；
  • ③ 一个好的睡眠习惯。
要求：要点齐全、时态正确（介绍习惯用一般现在时）、用上衔接词、书写规范。开头结尾已给。

Dear Mike,
Thank you for your letter. Now let me tell you how I keep fit.
（在此续写主体，约 60 词）

Please write to me soon.
Yours,
Li Hua

答案与解析（含写作四步示范 + 范文）
① 审题：对象 Mike，介绍"我怎么保持健康"＝讲习惯→用一般现在时、第一人称 I。
② 列点（确保不漏）：运动(play basketball twice a week / go swimming on Sundays)；饮食(eat vegetables & fruit, little junk food)；睡眠(go to bed early, sleep nine hours)。
③ 连句成段：用 First / Second / Also 把三点串起来。
④ 检查：三单 -s（he/she 才加，这里主语是 I 不加）、时态统一、首字母大写。
参考范文：
Dear Mike,
Thank you for your letter. Now let me tell you how I keep fit.
First, I do sports often. I play basketball twice a week and I go swimming every Sunday. Second, I eat well. I have lots of vegetables and fruit, and I don't eat much junk food. Also, I go to bed early and sleep for about nine hours every night. These good habits make me healthy and happy.
Please write to me soon.
Yours,
Li Hua
（评分看：三点是否齐全 + 衔接词是否连贯 + 一般现在时/人称是否对 + 卷面。范文用了 First/Second/Also 衔接，频度 twice a week/every Sunday/every night，均为一般现在时。）
