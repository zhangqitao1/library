二、核心语法速查（★期末必背）

2.1 特殊疑问句进阶（Unit 1）

  • 结构：疑问词 + 一般疑问句语序 → What do you like? / Why do you like cats?
  • 常用疑问词：what / which / who / where / when / why / how
  • 问数量/频率：how many（可数）｜ how much（不可数）｜ how often（频率）

2.2 形容词（Unit 1）

  • 作定语：形容词 + 名词 → a cute panda
  • 作表语：主语 + be + 形容词 → The tiger is dangerous.
  • 系表结构：look / feel / sound + 形容词 → The cat looks cute.
  • 常用：cute / friendly / shy / scary / dangerous / smart / lazy / active
⚠️ 形容词/副词比较级、最高级是八年级内容，不在七下范围，本卷不考（册地图与卡片均如此标注，不编造）。

2.3 祈使句与情态动词 have to / must（Unit 2 · 高频）

  • 祈使句：动词原形开头 → Be quiet. ｜ 否定 Don't + 原形 → Don't run.
  • have to（不得不，客观）：I have to… ｜ 三单 She has to… ｜ 否定 don't/doesn't have to（不必）
  • must（必须）：You must… ｜ 否定 mustn't（禁止，语气强）
  • 🔑 mustn't = 禁止（不许）≠ don't have to = 不必（可做可不做）
  • 情态动词后接动词原形（不加 to，不加 s）：✗ musts / mustn't to → ✓ must / mustn't

2.4 物主代词与频度副词（Unit 3）

  • 形容词性物主代词（后接名词）：my / your / his / her… → my book
  • 名词性物主代词（单独用）：mine / yours / his / hers… → This book is mine.
  • 频度副词：always > usually > often > sometimes > never；位置=be 后、实义动词前 → I often play…
  • 问频率用 How often…?；答 once / twice / three times a week

2.5 选择疑问句 + would like（Unit 4）

  • A or B? 直接选选项，不能答 Yes/No → Tea or coffee? — Coffee, please.
  • would like to do：Would you like to eat…? / I'd like a bowl of noodles.

2.6 可数与不可数名词进阶（Unit 4）

  • 可数用 how many + 复数；不可数用 how much（rice / water / milk / bread）
  • 不可数计量：a bowl of rice / a glass of water / a piece of bread
  • some（肯定句）/ any（否定、疑问）

2.7 现在进行时（Unit 5–6 · 高频）

  • 结构：am / is / are + v-ing → He is playing football.
  • -ing 变化：一般直接 +ing；去 e 加 ing（make→making）；重读闭音节双写末字母（run→running）
  • 标志词：now / right now / at the moment / Look! / Listen!
  • 疑问：What are you doing? ｜ 答 Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.

2.8 一般现在时 vs 现在进行时辨析（Unit 6 · 七下头号辨析）

一般现在时现在进行时
意义习惯、规律、客观事实此刻正在进行、现阶段暂时
结构动词原形 / 三单 +sam/is/are + v-ing
标志词always, usually, often, every day/weeknow, right now, at the moment, Look!
例句It rains a lot in summer.It is raining now.
⚠️ 状态动词不用进行时：know / like / love / want / need / have(拥有) → ✗ I am knowing ✓ I know

2.9 一般过去时（Unit 7–8 · 高频）

  • 规则动词：+ed（play→played）；辅音字母+y 变 i 加 ed（study→studied）
  • be 过去式：I/he/she/it → was（wasn't）；you/we/they → were（weren't）
  • 常见不规则：go→went, see→saw, do→did, have→had, eat→ate, come→came, take→took, get→got, make→made, buy→bought, think→thought, read→read /red/
  • 否定/疑问：实义动词借 did/didn't + 原形（Did you visit? / I didn't go.）；be 动词直接 was/were
  • 标志词：yesterday / last week / … ago / just now
  • 讲故事：Once upon a time, there was… One day, he went… first / then / finally

五、期末猜题（高频考点预测，非真题）

英语七下 · 期末自测卷

一、单项选择（每题2分，共16分）
1. — ___ do you like pandas? — Because they are cute. [特殊疑问句·基础]
A. What
B. Why
C. Where
D. When
答案与解析
B
答语 Because… 是说原因，问原因用 Why。
2. The tiger is very ___. Don't get close to it. [形容词·基础]
A. danger
B. dangerous
C. friend
D. friendly
答案与解析
B
be 后用形容词作表语；"危险的"是 dangerous（danger 是名词）。
3. You ___ talk loudly in the library. It's a rule. [情态动词·基础]
A. mustn't
B. don't have to
C. can
D. would
答案与解析
A
图书馆"禁止"大声说话用 mustn't（禁止）；don't have to 是"不必"，意思不对。
4. — Whose racket is this? — It's ___. I bought it last week. [物主代词·基础]
A. my
B. me
C. mine
D. I
答案与解析
C
单独使用、后面不接名词，用名词性物主代词 mine（=my racket）。
5. — Tea or coffee? — ___ [选择疑问句·基础]
A. Yes, please.
B. No, thanks.
C. Coffee, please.
D. I'd like.
答案与解析
C
选择疑问句不能答 Yes/No，要直接说选项。
6. Look! The children ___ in the playground now. [现在进行时·基础]
A. play
B. plays
C. are playing
D. played
答案与解析
C
Look! + now 是现在进行时标志，用 are + v-ing；children 是复数。
7. It often ___ in summer here, but look, it ___ now. [时态辨析·中档]
A. rains; is raining
B. is raining; rains
C. rains; rains
D. is raining; is raining
答案与解析
A
often→习惯，一般现在时 rains（三单 it）；look…now→此刻，现在进行时 is raining。
8. — How much milk do you need? — ___ [可数不可数·压轴]
A. Two milks.
B. Two glasses of milk.
C. Two bottle of milk.
D. Many milk.
答案与解析
B
milk 不可数，不能 milks，也不能 many milk；计量用"数词+量词复数+of+milk"，two glasses of milk（C 的 bottle 未变复数，错）。
二、用所给词的适当形式填空（每题2分，共12分）
9. She ___ (have) to wear a school uniform every day. [have to 三单·基础]
答案与解析
has
主语 She 是三单，have to → has to。
10. Listen! Someone ___ (sing) in the next room. [现在进行时·基础]
答案与解析
is singing
Listen! 表此刻，用现在进行时；someone 当三单，用 is。
11. They ___ (study) English every morning. [一般现在时·基础]
答案与解析
study
every morning→习惯，一般现在时；they 不是三单，用原形 study。
12. We ___ (go) to the zoo and ___ (see) pandas yesterday. [一般过去时·不规则·中档]
答案与解析
went; saw
yesterday→一般过去时；go→went，see→saw（不规则）。
13. ___ you ___ (visit) your grandparents last weekend? [过去时一般疑问·中档]
答案与解析
Did; visit
过去时疑问句借助 Did，其后动词用原形 visit（不是 visited）。
14. Once upon a time, there ___ (be) a kind king. He ___ (have) three sons. [讲故事·be/不规则·压轴]
答案与解析
was; had
讲故事用过去时；there be 单数 a king→was；have→had（不规则）。
三、句型转换（每题2分，共6分）
15. He plays football after school.（改为现在进行时，加 now） [时态转换·中档]
答案与解析
He is playing football now.
一般现在时→现在进行时：is + v-ing；plays→is playing。
16. I went to Beijing last summer.（改为一般疑问句） [过去时转疑问·基础]
答案与解析
Did you go to Beijing last summer?
过去时一般疑问句：Did + 主语 + 动词原形；went→go，I→you。
17. You must be quiet in class.（改为祈使句，否定命令"别说话"） [祈使句·基础]
答案与解析
Don't talk in class.
否定祈使句用 Don't + 动词原形（参考易错点 ✗Not run → ✓Don't run）。
四、完成句子 / 汉译英（每题3分，共9分）
18. 你多久锻炼一次？（用 how often） [频率提问·中档]
答案与解析
How often do you exercise?
问频率用 How often + 一般疑问句语序。
19. 这盘子里有一些面包，但你不必全吃完。（some / a piece of / don't have to） [综合·不可数+不必·压轴]
答案与解析
There is some bread on the plate, but you don't have to eat it all.
bread 不可数用 some（或 a piece of bread）；"不必"用 don't have to（不是 mustn't）。表达接近即可。
五、短文填空（用括号词的适当形式，每空2分，共12分）
20. 用所给词的正确形式填空： [综合时态语篇·中档]

Last Sunday ___(1) (be) a great day. My family ___(2) (go) to the park. The weather ___(3) (be) sunny. Now I ___(4) (write) about it. Look! My little brother ___(5) (play) with the dog, and my parents ___(6) (talk) under a big tree.

答案与解析
(1) was　(2) went　(3) was　(4) am writing　(5) is playing　(6) are talking
Last Sunday→过去时：was / went（go 不规则）/ was；Now、Look!→现在进行时：am writing / is playing（brother 三单）/ are talking（parents 复数）。这道题正是"过去时 + 现在进行时"混合，先找每句的时间标志词再定时态。
