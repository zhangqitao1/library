一、英语期末有哪些题型（先知己知彼）

  • 听力（本卷略，平时多听天气预报、点餐、打电话对话，听清关键词与 or 两边选项）。
  • 单项选择：考时态、情态动词、可数不可数、物主代词、疑问词——本册易错点的主战场。
  • 完形填空：一段短文挖空，靠上下文语境选，不是单看一个空。先读懂整段大意再做。
  • 阅读理解：抓主旨、找细节、猜词义，先看题干再回原文定位。
  • 词汇运用 / 语法填空：用所给词的适当形式填空（时态、词性、单复数）。
  • 书面表达：按本册话题写一段（动物 / 规则 / 健康 / 饮食 / 难忘一天 / 故事），用对时态、套范文句型。

单元话题目标语法必记口诀 / 结构

一般现在时现在进行时一般过去时

三、高频词汇短语与易错点（考前最后扫一遍）

易错对照✗ 错✓ 对
特殊疑问句缺助动词What you like?What do you like?
形容词 vs 名词She is friend.She is friendly.
否定祈使句Not run! / No run!Don't run!
禁止 vs 不必mustn't（想表"不必"）禁止→mustn't；不必→don't have to
名词性物主代词This book is my.This book is mine.
频度副词位置I play often basketball.I often play basketball.
选择疑问句回答— Tea or coffee? — Yes.— Coffee, please.
不可数名词two rices / a breadtwo bowls of rice / a piece of bread
much / manyHow many waterHow much water
现在进行时构成He is play. / She is runing.He is playing. / She is running.
时态混用He is playing every day.He plays every day.
过去时否定/疑问I didn't went. / Did you visited?I didn't go. / Did you visit?
不规则过去式She goed.She went.
be 动词过去式They was happy.They were happy.
天气表达It's sun. / weather areIt's sunny. / weather is
⚠️ 万能三步法：① 先找标志词定时态（now→进行 / yesterday→过去 / every day→一般现在）；② 助动词 do/does/did/can/must/have to 后一律用动词原形；③ 选择疑问句别答 Yes，直接说选项。

各单元高频短语速记

  • 动物/健康：be good for, keep fit, stay healthy, do exercise, go swimming / running, once / twice a week, hardly ever。
  • 规则/饮食：have to, must / mustn't, on time, follow the rules; would like to, a bowl/glass/piece of, How much/many。
  • 天气/活动：How's the weather?, It's sunny/rainy/cloudy/windy/snowy, go hiking, fly kites, make snowmen。
  • 过去/故事：last weekend, … ago, How was your trip?, Once upon a time, one day, first / then / finally, in the end。

一、单项选择（每题1分，共12分）
1. — ______ animals do you like best? — I like dolphins because they are smart. [考点：特殊疑问词 what（难度：基础）]
A. What
B. How
C. When
D. Why
答案与解析
A
问"什么动物"用 What animals。答句说的是喜欢哪种动物（信息），不是地点/时间/原因，所以选 A。
2. The tiger is a ______ animal, so we must be careful in the zoo. [考点：形容词 vs 名词（难度：基础）]
A. danger
B. dangerous
C. dangerously
D. dangers
答案与解析
B
修饰名词 animal 要用形容词 dangerous。danger 是名词"危险"，dangerously 是副词，都不能直接修饰名词。
3. — Can I bring my phone to school? — No, you ______. It's against the school rules. [考点：mustn't 表禁止（难度：中档）]
A. don't have to
B. mustn't
C. needn't
D. may not have to
答案与解析
B
"违反校规、不许带"是禁止，用 mustn't。don't have to / needn't 都表示"不必"（可带可不带），不符合"against the rules"。
4. You ______ wear a uniform on weekends. You can wear your own clothes. [考点：don't have to 表不必（难度：中档）]
A. mustn't
B. can't
C. don't have to
D. must
答案与解析
C
后一句"可以穿自己的衣服"说明周末"不必"穿校服，用 don't have to。mustn't 是"禁止穿"，意思相反。这是 mustn't 与 don't have to 的经典辨析。
5. This racket isn't ______. It's my sister's. [考点：名词性物主代词（难度：基础）]
A. my
B. me
C. mine
D. I
答案与解析
C
be 动词后、句末没有名词，要用名词性物主代词 mine（=my racket）。my 后面必须接名词，排除。
6. — ______ do you go running? — Three times a week. [考点：How often 问频率（难度：基础）]
A. How long
B. How often
C. How much
D. How far
答案与解析
B
答语"一周三次"是频率，用 How often 提问。How long 问时长，How far 问距离，都对不上。
7. — Would you like some rice ______ noodles? — Noodles, please. [考点：选择疑问句 or（难度：基础）]
A. and
B. or
C. but
D. with
答案与解析
B
答句直接说选项 Noodles，说明是"二选一"的选择疑问句，用 or 连接。
8. There isn't ______ milk in the fridge. Let's go and buy some. [考点：any 用于否定 + 不可数（难度：中档）]
A. some
B. many
C. any
D. a
答案与解析
C
否定句用 any；milk 不可数，不能用 many 或 a。some 一般用于肯定句。
9. Listen! Someone ______ in the next room. [考点：现在进行时·Listen!（难度：基础）]
A. sings
B. sang
C. is singing
D. sing
答案与解析
C
Listen! 是现在进行时的标志词，表示此刻正在进行，用 am/is/are + v-ing。
10. It often ______ in Jinan in summer, but look, it ______ now. [考点：一般现在时 vs 现在进行时（难度：压轴）]
A. rains; is raining
B. is raining; rains
C. rains; rains
D. is raining; is raining
答案与解析
A
前半句 often 表习惯→一般现在时 rains（it 三单）；后半句 look + now 表此刻→现在进行时 is raining。一句话两个时态，是七下头号辨析。
11. — How ______ your weekend? — It ______ great. I went to the beach. [考点：be 动词过去式（难度：中档）]
A. is; is
B. was; was
C. were; were
D. was; were
答案与解析
B
问的是过去的周末（后文 went 也是过去时），主语 weekend / it 都是单数，用 was。
12. Yesterday my brother ______ to the zoo and ______ many photos. [考点：一般过去时·不规则动词（难度：压轴）]
A. goes; takes
B. went; took
C. went; takes
D. goed; taked
答案与解析
B
yesterday 提示一般过去时，go→went、take→took 都是不规则动词。goed / taked 是错误拼写，排除 D。
二、完形填空（每题1分，共8分）
阅读下面短文，从每题 A、B、C 三个选项中选出最佳答案。 [考点：语境综合·时态与词汇（难度：基础6中档2）]
Last Saturday was a special day for Lucy. In the morning, the weather (13)______ sunny and warm, so she decided to visit her grandparents in the countryside. She (14)______ there by bus with her mother.
Her grandparents were very happy to see them. Grandma cooked a big lunch. "Would you like some rice (15)______ noodles?" Grandma asked. "Noodles, please," Lucy said. The food (16)______ delicious.
After lunch, Lucy (17)______ in the garden with her grandpa. Suddenly it started to rain. "Look! It (18)______ now," Grandpa said. So they ran back into the house and played chess. Lucy (19)______ chess very much.
In the evening, they went home. Lucy was tired but happy. It really (20)______ a day to remember.
13. A. is    B. was    C. are
14. A. goes    B. go    C. went
15. A. or    B. and    C. but
16. A. were    B. was    C. is
17. A. plays    B. is playing    C. played
18. A. rains    B. is raining    C. rained
19. A. likes    B. liked    C. is liking
20. A. was    B. were    C. is
答案与解析
13. B   14. C   15. A   16. B   17. C   18. B   19. B   20. A
全文讲"上周六"的经历，主线是一般过去时。
13. 主语 weather 单数 + 过去 → was。
14. 过去动作"坐车去"→ went（不规则）。
15. 选择疑问句 rice ___ noodles，答语只选一个 → or。
16. food 不可数当单数 + 过去 → was。
17. after lunch 过去动作 → played。
18. "Look! …now"此刻正在下雨 → is raining（进行时，作者直接引语）。
19. like 是状态动词不用进行时，整体过去 → liked。
20. "a day to remember"对全天的总结，过去 + it 单数 → was。
三、阅读理解（每题2分，共10分）
阅读短文，判断 21–22 题正误（T/F），选择 23–25 题。 [考点：细节理解 + 主旨 + 猜词（难度：基础3中档2）]
Tom is a middle school student. He has many good habits. He gets up at six thirty every day and does morning exercises. He thinks it is good for his health.
Tom plays sports a lot. He plays basketball twice a week and goes swimming on weekends. He hardly ever drinks cola because it is unhealthy. For breakfast, he usually eats two eggs, some bread and a glass of milk.
But today is different. It is Sunday and it is raining outside, so Tom can't go swimming. Right now he is staying at home. He is reading an interesting story about a brave king. "Once upon a time, there was a king…" He really enjoys it.

21. Tom does morning exercises every day. ( ______ )

22. Tom goes swimming twice a week. ( ______ )

23. How often does Tom play basketball?

A. Every day.    B. Twice a week.    C. On weekends.    D. Hardly ever.

24. Why isn't Tom swimming today?

A. Because he is ill.    B. Because it is raining.    C. Because he is reading.    D. Because it is too cold.

25. The underlined word "unhealthy" means ______ in Chinese.

A. 健康的    B. 不健康的    C. 美味的    D. 便宜的
答案与解析
21. T   22. F   23. B   24. B   25. B
21. 第一段"does morning exercises every day"→正确 T。
22. 原文 plays basketball twice a week，swimming 是 on weekends，把篮球频率安到游泳上→错误 F（细节偷换，常见陷阱）。
23. 直接定位"plays basketball twice a week"→B。
24. "it is raining… so Tom can't go swimming"→B。
25. un- 前缀表否定，healthy 健康→unhealthy 不健康的，且上文说 cola 所以 hardly ever drinks，能反推词义→B。
四、词汇运用 / 语法填空（每题1分，共8分）
用所给词的适当形式填空（每空一词，可能涉及时态、词性、单复数）。
26. Look! The children ______ (play) football on the playground. [考点：现在进行时·Look!（难度：基础）]
答案与解析
are playing
Look! → 现在进行时；主语 children 复数用 are + playing。
27. My mother ______ (not have) to work last Sunday, so we went hiking. [考点：过去时否定·have to（难度：中档）]
答案与解析
didn't have
last Sunday → 一般过去时；have to 的过去否定是 didn't have to + 原形。
28. I'd like two ______ (bowl) of rice, please. [考点：量词复数（难度：基础）]
答案与解析
bowls
two 后量词用复数 bowls；rice 不可数本身不变。two bowls of rice。
29. How ______ (much / many) eggs do you eat every morning? [考点：many 修饰可数（难度：基础）]
答案与解析
many
eggs 是可数名词复数，用 How many。（milk/water 那类不可数才用 much）
30. She is my best friend. We see ______ (she / her) every day. [考点：宾格代词（难度：中档）]
答案与解析
her
动词 see 后面作宾语，用宾格 her。
31. Don't ______ (run) in the hallways. It's dangerous. [考点：否定祈使句·动词原形（难度：基础）]
答案与解析
run
否定祈使句 Don't + 动词原形，run 保持原形。
32. Yesterday she ______ (study) English for two hours. [考点：规则动词过去式拼写（难度：中档）]
答案与解析
studied
yesterday → 过去时；study 辅音字母 + y，变 y 为 i 再 +ed → studied。
33. How's the weather today? — It's ______ (sun) and warm. [考点：名词变形容词（难度：基础）]
答案与解析
sunny
问天气答语用形容词，sun → sunny（双写 n + y）。It's sunny，不能说 It's sun。
五、书面表达（共10分）
34. 假设上周末你度过了难忘的一天。请以 "A Day to Remember" 为题，写一篇 60–80 词的短文。
要求：① 用一般过去时；② 写清时间、地点、和谁、做了什么、感受；③ 可参考词：went, visited, took photos, wonderful。 [考点：一般过去时综合写作·难忘一天（难度：中档+压轴）]
参考范文与提示
A Day to Remember
参考范文：
Last Sunday was a day to remember. In the morning, I went to the zoo with my parents. The weather was sunny and warm. We saw many animals there, such as pandas, tigers and dolphins. The pandas were so cute! We took a lot of photos and had a big lunch together. In the afternoon, we played games in the park. I was a little tired, but I felt very happy. It was a wonderful day!

评分自检：① 全篇一般过去时（was / went / saw / took / had / played / felt）有没有用对？② 第三人称、单复数对不对？③ 时间→地点→人物→做什么→感受 五要素齐不齐？④ 有没有用上 because / and / so / such as 这类连接词让句子更连贯？
